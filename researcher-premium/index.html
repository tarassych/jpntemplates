Most common questions

How much does a Premium Membership cost?

A Premium Membership is free for you to try for a full year. After that it costs USD $30/year. At the end of your free trial period you will be given the option to upgrade. There are no automatic billing or renewals.

How do the discounts work?

Members who sign up for Premium are sent coupons via email. Coupons are provided at various intervals throughout the year. Unless stated otherwise, coupons may be used to pay for all services on www.edanz.com.

What do I get access to?

You get unrestricted access to all of our premium material: courses, tools, and other special offers! All of your premium content may be found on your My Account page.

Can I cancel?

Absolutely. You may cancel your membership any time, and you'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period. You can cancel at any time by visiting your Account page.
