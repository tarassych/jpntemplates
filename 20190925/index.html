Do you know the difference between...

“Poisonous” “Venomous”

Image goes here
解 説

The terms "venomous" and "poisonous" are often used interchangeably to refer to toxic substances produced by animals and their dangers to humans and other creatures, but they have different meanings in biology. Basically, venoms are delivered actively while poisons are delivered passively.
