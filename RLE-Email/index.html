So you’ve selected a journal, checked its requirements (Episode 1), and started your first draft (Episode 2). Now your manuscript is really beginning to take shape! So prepare yourself for our third episode in the Early Career Academics series.

You’re invited to see an expert analysis uncovering the most surprising, yet common mistakes many authors make. This one hour interactive event could save you hours, days, or even weeks of wasted time revising and resubmitting! As always, please bring your questions!
