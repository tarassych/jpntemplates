Literature Reviews & Data Extraction

Is there a question you need answered but don't have the time to search through all of the literature? Do you require assistance in making sense of enormous amounts of data? Our team can help you conduct a comprehensive and unbiased review. In addition, we can also provide trend reports, quantitative analyses, and qualitative analyses. Together, these services can give you a well-rounded view of the current state of research in your field and help you to identify potential areas for further study.

Systematic Literature Review

Is there a topic in your field that warrants a systematic review? We can help you conceptualize, design and execute the review: search parameters, inclusion/exclusion criteria, sorting and filtering, providing assessments on the evidence quality level according to standardized scales. We can help summarise the literature according to thematic criteria, provide interpretation of what the body of evidence suggests, as well as speculate on what might be coming next.

  • You provide the vision, we’ll do the heavy lifting!
  • Interpretation and assessment support available throughout
  • Get known in your field by providing a top-down view of your chosen topic
