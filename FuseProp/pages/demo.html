App coming Q3 2023

Manager logs into the app and selects the applicable property and unit #.

Using the default inspection template, the manager clicks into the relevant sections to upload photos of damage…

… and can assign rooms as appropriate with any helpful notes.

All inspections are saved to the cloud and can be used to generate summaries for statements of deposit (SODAs).

Expanded Browse coming Q3 2023

Manager begins creating PO with # and property and can search/add products from a pre-curated library.

Manager adds unit(s) to the PO from the auto-complete dropdown and assigns materials to the corresponding rooms.

Manager reviews order summary before submitting for approvals as necessary.

Approvals coming Q4 2023

The PO with approval status is visible on the shared dashboard, including access to photos from the inspection.

Once the PO is approved, the manager requests an installation date, triggering an email to the vendor.

Vendor clicks into the platform to approve the installation date (or to propose an alt date), triggering a confirmation email to the manager.

All install details including material selections, quantities and the floor plan are visible in the vendor’s dashboard.

Vendor’s installer inputs the PO# from their ticket and confirms the work order.

Prior to commencing any work, the vendor’s installer walks the unit and reports any damage with photos.

Upon completing the work, the vendor’s installer submits photos documenting the installation.

Vendor submits details for change order.

Asset Manager is immediately notified and can approve the change order directly from the email or from within their dashboard.

Upon approval, the PO is updated and the vendor is notified to proceed.
