Inspection

Manager logs into the app.

Manager selects property and unit #.

Using the default inspection template, the manager clicks into the relevant sections to upload photos of damage…

… and can create / assign tasks as appropriate indicating the action to be taken, whether it’s billable to the tenant and other helpful information.

All inspections are saved to the cloud and can be accessed from any computer or mobile device with an internet connection.

Supporting documentation for statements of deposit (SODAs) are always a click away, and tasks are ready to feed into our PO platform or your own PO platform.

PO Request

Manager begins creating PO with # and property.

Expanded Browse coming Q2 2024

A pre-curated library of products are readily available to drop into POs.

Manager adds unit(s) to the PO from the auto-complete dropdown.

Manager assigns materials to the corresponding rooms.

Manager reviews order summary before submitting for approvals as necessary.

An email is immediately sent to those stakeholders that need to approve the PO.

Approvals coming Q2 2024

The PO, with approval status, is visible on the shared dashboard, including access to photos from the inspection.

Scheduling

Once the PO is approved, the manager requests an installation date.

An email is immediately sent to the vendor.

Vendor clicks into the platform to approve the installation date or to propose an alt date.

A confirmation email is immediately sent to the manager.

All install details, including material selections and quantities, are visible in the vendor’s dashboard.

The floor plan is available too.

Install

Vendor’s installer inputs the PO# from their ticket.

Prior to commencing any work, the vendor’s installer walks the unit and reports any damage with photos.

Upon completing the work, the vendor’s installer submits photos documenting the installation.

Change Order

Vendor submits details for change order.

Asset Manager is immediately notified and can approve the change order directly from the email…

… or from within their dashboard.

Upon approval, the PO is updated.

The vendor is immediately notified to proceed too.
