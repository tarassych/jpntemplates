Welcome to DesignerPages.com! Here are some quick tips for getting started:

  1. Find Products by browsing within the Category menu or Brands Index, or by searching for the product, brand or category you want! Search smarter by using filters available on Category pages marked with a SMART flag (like Carpet) in the category menu.
  2. Browse Product Index Pages more effectively by mousing over products for a quick view - with product name, brand name and more product images! Move your mouse to the bottom of the image until you see numbers - then scroll left and right to view more images.
  3. Make the Most of Product Data by viewing product images, checking out the product specifications and description, and even downloading attachments (such as CAD files) located at the bottom of the product page.
  4. Save Your Favorites - products, brands and categories - so they're easily accessible whenever you're logged into your DesignerPages.com account. Just click the star icon on any product or brand page and it will show up in Your Favorites immediately.
