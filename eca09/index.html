Welcome to Episode 9 -- FUNDING SECRETS for Researchers!

From early career researchers to seasoned principal investigators, writing a grant proposal to get research funding can be a terrifying task. However, you don’t have to do it alone—we at Edanz are here to support you!

Edanz’s Scott McCleary welcomes our special mystery guest funding expert “Doctor T” for an interactive and information-packed hour of tips and tricks.

Doctor T will highlight some of his top, time-tested techniques for getting funding for YOUR next research project, including:

・the key ingredients to a successful grant proposal: significance, investigators, innovation, approach, environment...
・the ‘secret sauce’ of a grant proposal: summary, metrics and measures of esteem, impact summary, impact pathway, scientific illustration...
・how to get into the correct (=confident!) mindset for writing a killer proposal that will turn reviewers into ADVOCATES!

Join us! As always, we invite YOUR questions and comments about funding. See you there!

“Doctor T” (special mystery guest expert!)


Doctor T's science communications began as a writer with Nature Publishing Group, then as an editor at the National Children's Hospital in Japan, and later as an editor and educator with Edanz. He has worked closely with multinational corporations, national hospitals, and universities to advise clinicians and researchers on grant application and publishing strategies. He holds a PhD in Medicine and Health Sciences from the University of Melbourne, Australia.

Scott McCleary (host / moderator)


Scott specializes in online instructional design for lifelong learning. He has developed and delivered online training programs and products for more than 50 organizations worldwide in fields such as medicine & pharma, business & finance, law, technology, government, and higher education. Scott is the creator of Edanz Learning Lab (learning.edanz.com).
